Monster Survivors

The ultimate free-to-play survival game where you face down hundreds of monsters at once.

About The Game

Dive into a relentless onslaught in Monster Survivors, an action-packed, minimalist survival game. The odds are stacked against you, with hordes of creatures closing in from all sides. There is nowhere to hide. All you can do is survive, gather experience crystals from fallen enemies, and evolve your arsenal into a devastating force of destruction. How long can you last before the inevitable end?

How to Play

The controls are brilliantly simple, designed for pure action focus. Your character attacks automatically, leaving you to concentrate on the most important thing: movement and strategic positioning.

  • Movement: Use your mouse, WASD keys, or arrow keys to dodge enemies and projectiles.
  • Upgrades: When you level up, you'll be presented with new weapons and passive skills. Choose wisely to build a powerful synergy and create your ultimate monster-slaying machine.

Key Features

Monster Survivors is designed for endless fun and replayability. Each run is a new challenge.

  • One-vs-Thousands: Experience the thrill of single-handedly obliterating massive waves of enemies.
  • Deep Progression: Unlock dozens of weapons, skills, and characters, each offering a unique gameplay experience.
  • Endless Replayability: With randomized upgrades and enemy patterns, no two runs are ever the same. Master the chaos and challenge your limits.
